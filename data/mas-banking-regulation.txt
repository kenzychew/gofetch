Banking

Deposit-taking institutions in Singapore operate as full banks, wholesale banks, merchant banks or finance companies. Find out about the regulations, updates and licensing for such institutions.

Types of Deposit-Taking Institutions

Singapore has different types of deposit-taking institutions with different permitted activities:
- Full banks
- Wholesale banks
- Merchant banks
- Finance companies

Licensing and Authorisation for Banking Business

Find out how to apply for a wholesale banking licence or to operate as a merchant bank.

Set up Bank Representative Office

Find out how to set up a bank representative office in Singapore.

Digital Bank Licence

Singapore introduced a digital bank licence framework to allow non-traditional players to operate digital banks. This framework enables innovative banking solutions while maintaining financial stability.

Compliance Toolkits

MAS provides compliance toolkits that aim to guide and facilitate banks' understanding and compliance with the various MAS approval, notification, and reporting requirements and timelines.

Third-Party Risk Management

MAS sets requirements and expectations for how financial institutions should manage risks arising from third party and outsourcing arrangements.

Key regulatory guidelines:
- Guidelines on Outsourcing for Banks
- FAQ on Outsourcing Guidelines
- Guidelines to Notice 626 on Prevention of Money Laundering and Countering the Financing of Terrorism (CFT) for Banks
- Guidelines on Risk Management Practices - Internal Controls
- Interest Rate Benchmarks Transition
- Notice 610 (Submission of Statistics and Returns)
- Notice 609 (Minimum Cash Balances)

Financial Institutions Directory

MAS maintains a directory of all licensed financial institutions operating in Singapore.

Regulations and Guidance

The MAS Act confers on MAS powers to issue legal instruments for regulating and supervising financial institutions.

Development Initiatives

MAS offers tax incentives and grant schemes for financial institutions with plans to establish or expand their operations in Singapore, as well as training programmes for financial sector professionals.
